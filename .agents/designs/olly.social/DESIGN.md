# Design System: Olly Social

## Overview
Olly is a playful, energetic AI engagement agent for social media. The aesthetic is fast, friendly, and highly interactive.

## Design Philosophy
- **Social Energy:** Use vibrant colors and gradients to mimic the speed and emotion of social platforms.
- **Human Connection:** Soft, rounded UI components (chat-bubble inspired) make the AI feel approachable.
- **Micro-Interactions:** Focus on energetic feedback for user actions.

## Colors
- **Signature Rose (`#F43F5E`):** Represents passion and social engagement. 
- **Sunset Gradient:** Used for hero sections and primary buttons to convey energy and growth.

## Components
- **Chat Bubbles:** High border radius (16px) with distinct tails or asymmetrical rounding.
- **Action Buttons:** Vibrant rose-to-orange gradients.
- **Feed Cards:** Minimalist, focusing on content with large rounded corners.
