# Design System: BGRemover Video

## Overview
BGRemover Video is a professional-grade creative studio tool for background removal. It follows the "Dark Mode by Default" pattern of high-end video editing software.

## Design Philosophy
- **Pro Creativity:** Dark interface reduces eye strain and makes video content pop.
- **Precision Engineering:** Blue accents represent the mathematical precision of background isolation.
- **High-Tech Aesthetic:** Transparent grid patterns and "floating" UI panels.

## Colors
- **Precision Blue (`#3B82F6`):** The primary color for "isolated" or "active" states.
- **Ebony Surfaces:** Deep blacks and dark greys (`#09090B`) to provide the ultimate backdrop for video editing.

## Components
- **Video Controls:** Small, precise icons with Blue active states.
- **Timeline Rails:** Minimalist grey bars with Blue playheads.
- **Preview Canvas:** Checkered transparent background grid.
- **Toolbar:** Floating glassmorphic panels with `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`.
