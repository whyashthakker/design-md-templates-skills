# Design System: Infloq

## Overview
Infloq is the enterprise hub of the suite, focused on trust, intelligence, and ROI for influencer marketing. The style is authoritative, clean, and data-rich.

## Design Philosophy
- **Authoritative Clarity:** Use white space and strong typography to convey trust.
- **Data-First:** ROI and metrics should be presented with high-legibility mono fonts.
- **Enterprise Polish:** Large border radii and soft shadows create a premium, modern SaaS feel.

## Colors
- **Primary Indigo (`#6366F1`):** Represents intelligence and professional networking. Used for primary CTAs and key brand moments.
- **Slate Neutrals:** Used for secondary text and borders to maintain a professional, low-distraction environment.

## Components
- **Dashboard Cards:** 12px border radius with very soft ambient shadows.
- **Primary Buttons:** Indigo gradients with 8px rounding.
- **Data Tables:** Clean, minimalist borders with JetBrains Mono for numeric values.
